Adopt a Corner

Step 1 of 3
Claim a block.
Make it yours.
The Adopt a Corner program lets any resident or group claim a street corner on the Mid-Market corridor. Show up Saturdays at noon, bring your crew, and build something the neighborhood wants to stop at.
What you're committing to
📅
Show up Saturdays at noonBring your crew every week and keep the corner active and welcoming.
🧹
Keep it clean and safeThe corner is yours — its condition reflects on the whole program.
📸
Document the first 30 daysSubmit 3+ photos of your setup to The Pulse and the Mid-Market Foundation within 30 days of submission.
📋
File required permits within 30 daysPermanent structures, public art, and group gatherings each need city permits. We'll send you the right forms.
🤝
Be a good neighborEngage the block, respect businesses, and leave the space better than you found it.
What you receive
💰
$50/month maintenance allowanceCity-backed grant paid monthly once your corner is approved.
🎨
BLICK Art Materials starter kitUV paint, chalk sets, or garden tools — your choice based on corner theme.
📑
Streamlined permit processingThe Mid-Market Foundation coordinates with SF DPW on your behalf.
📍
Listed on The PulseYour corner gets its own card, QR code, and map pin on this dashboard.

What happens next
✅
Application received
Your preliminary application is with the Mid-Market Foundation and SF Civic Center. Check your email for confirmation.
📅
Activate your corner
Set up your space, get your crew out on a Saturday, and start documenting. You need at least 3 photos showing the corner active and in use.
30-day window from today
📋
File your permits
The Foundation will email you the specific permit forms for your corner type. File with SF DPW within the 30-day window — they'll coordinate on your behalf.
🔄
City review
Once your photos and permits are filed, SF City Planning reviews your activation. Typical turnaround is 5–10 business days.
🎉
Approval — you're official
Your corner goes live on The Pulse with its own card and QR code. Your $50/month allowance begins, and your BLICK starter kit ships within 5 business days.
