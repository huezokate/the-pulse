The Program

Adopt a Corner.
Make it yours.

Residents claim a street corner on Market Street, show up every Saturday at noon, and collectively turn it into something the whole block can feel. No experience required — just show up.

$50/month city grant reimbursement
BLICK Art Materials activation kit on approval
Your corner gets a card, QR code & map pin on The Pulse
Partnered with Mid-Market Foundation & Urban Alchemy
1
Read the Guidelines
Community principles, safety expectations, and a list of what BLICK stocks. Takes 5 minutes — sets you up for the conversation.
2
File a Preliminary Application
Choose your corner, describe your vision, name your crew. Goes to Civic Center for a 4–6 week review. You'll hear back with next steps and permit guidance.
3
Activate Every Saturday
One month to set up, document, and file permits. Then you're live — meetup at noon, post photos, appear on The Pulse. Your corner becomes community infrastructure.
